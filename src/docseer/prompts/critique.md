Critically analyze the selected papers:

1. **Assumptions** — what assumptions do the authors make? Are they justified?
2. **Methodology concerns** — are there methodological flaws or limitations?
3. **Evidence strength** — how strong is the empirical evidence? Are claims well-supported?
4. **Reproducibility** — could the results be reproduced? Are details sufficient?
5. **Alternative explanations** — are there other interpretations of the results?
6. **Open questions** — what questions remain unanswered?
